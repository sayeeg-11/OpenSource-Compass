⚖️ Code of Conduct

Welcome to the OpenSource Compass Community. Together, we build a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for all contributors.

Our Pledge

We are dedicated to fostering a collaborative and inclusive environment for all contributors. Participation in this project will always remain harassment-free regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

What We Encourage

  • Showing empathy toward others
  • Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • Giving constructive feedback
  • Accepting responsibility for mistakes
  • Respectful dissent

What We Discourage (Zero Tolerance)

  • Harassment or discrimination
  • Inappropriate conduct
  • Confidentiality breaches
  • Trolling or derogatory remarks
  • Any act making others uncomfortable or unsafe

Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers commit to clarifying and enforcing community standards fairly. We lead by example, protect confidentiality, avoid bias, and ensure the project remains transparent and welcoming.

Our Commitment

We strive to build a space where every student feels empowered to grow their technical skills and share their ideas confidently. Thank you for making OpenSource Compass a better place.
